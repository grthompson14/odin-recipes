Coleslaw Dressing

\

This creamy coleslaw dressing is a delightful topper for all of your coleslaw dreams. You can pour it immediately over your veggie mix or make it ahead and refrigerate until needed.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together all ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  2. Use immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
