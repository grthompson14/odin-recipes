Greek Dressing

This quick and easy dressing can be paired with a variety of salads from a regular veggie delight to a pasta salad. This herby, tangy dressing can be made ahead, easily scaled down, and travels well since it doesn't have to stay refrigerated.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup garlic powder
  • 1/3 cup dried oregano
  • 1/3 cup dried basil
  • 1/4 cup black pepper
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup onion powder
  • 1/4 cup dijon mustard
  • 6 cups red wine vinegar

Directions

  1. Step 1 - Mix olive oil and dijon mustard with all of the dry ingredients in a large container.
  2. Step 2 - Pour vinegar in slowly while mixing until well-blended. Store in a tightly covered container at room temperature.
