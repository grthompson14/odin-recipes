Avocado Dressing

This avocado dressing is the perfect blend of creamy and tangy to accompany any salad or to switch things up this could be served as a delicious dip!

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Blend avocado, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, sea salt, hot sauce, and black pepper together in a blender until smooth.
